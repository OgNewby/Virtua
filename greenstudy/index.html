IELTS

IELTS is a very crucial element to apply for scholarships and study abroad! At a minimum, you need an IELTS score of 6.5 or more according to the provisions of the University and the intended major. To achieve a high score, proper preparation is needed. The IELTS® Preparation Class by Green Study is designed to optimize yourself with various tips for understanding and mastering the entire structure of the IELTS test (Reading, Listening, Writing and Speaking). .

TOEFL

Students who have received their education in non-English speaking countries are often required to demonstrate an acceptable level of English proficiency on the TOEFL in order to be considered for admission to American colleges and universities. TOEFL is ideally taken when a student is in between Year 10 and early Year 12 to make sure there is enough time for preparation. The TOEFL result itself is valid for 2 years since the date it is taken. What will you get from this course: • Learn the Best Test Taking Strategies Optimally and Efficiently • Improve your Skills in Speaking, Reading, Listening, Writing • Practice your English with Native Speaking Teacher

Cambridge Tests

The course will focus on task based achievement; what it means and how to achieve it. The key is understanding the reasoning behind a task in your exams to come. We will look into timing and planning as well as the Accuracy and appropriacy. The main skills tested are speaking, writing, listening and reading. Most importantly we will focus on the use and control of grammatical structures with a combination of improving and expanding lexical range. This is a 32 hr course, which is aimed for people who want to get their B1 certificate or Cambridge first..
